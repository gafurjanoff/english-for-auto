Lesson 30. Education to practice

TASK 1 30. Listen to the information and pay attention to the bolded words.
For automobile students, bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application is crucial.

1. Hands-on Workshops & Labs
Engage in practical sessions involving engine disassembly and assembly. Conduct diagnostic tests using OBD scanners. Work with 3D models and simulations of automotive systems.

2. Internships & Apprenticeships
Join automotive workshops, service centers, or manufacturing plants. Work under skilled mechanics and engineers to learn real-world problem-solving.

3. Project-Based Learning
Design and build miniature models of engines or transmission systems. Participate in electric vehicle (EV) conversions. Work on fuel efficiency optimization projects.
