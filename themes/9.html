COMPONENTS OF A CAR CONSTRUCTION
  • Dashboard. It is an elaborate “command center” that allows the driver to control many systems, such as air conditioning, and lights.
  • Dashboard is a place for icons and symbols, as well as the car .
  • Main car components. The design of a car includes many important systems, such as suspension, braking, steering, drive, , cooling and lighting.
  • Car body. The body consists of the supporting structure, bodywork, hull, and equipment.
  • Engine. Car construction and operating principle cannot be complete without an engine, that is, a machine that converts the energy supplied to the vehicle into energy. Depending on the type of propulsion system, the energy source can be gasoline, diesel, electricity and sometimes even hydrogen. The generation of mechanical energy allows the car to be put into motion.
