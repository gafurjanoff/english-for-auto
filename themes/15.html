LESSON 15. Lubrication system in an internal combustion engine

TASK 1 Read the information and define the bolded words
LUBRICATION SYSTEM COMPONENTS

A lubrication system in an internal combustion engine is a system that aids in lubricating and cooling the engine's moving parts. Its main purpose is to lessen friction between moving parts, which slows down the rate of wear and tear on these engine parts.
