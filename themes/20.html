Lesson 20. Gas distribution mechanism

TASK 1 Read and define the bolded words.

The gas distribution mechanism (also known as the valve timing mechanism) in a car is responsible for controlling the flow of air-fuel mixture into the engine cylinders and the expulsion of exhaust gases. This mechanism works in synchronization with the crankshaft to ensure efficient combustion and engine performance.
