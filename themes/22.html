Lesson 22. Monitoring and warning devices

TASK 1 Read and define the bolded words.

Cars have a variety of monitoring and warning devices to help keep drivers safe and alert. A safety feature that uses a camera to monitor the driver's behavior and alert them if they become distracted or drowsy. The camera can be located in the steering column or dashboard and uses infrared LEDs or lasers to see the driver's face.

Indicators on the dashboard that communicate important information about the vehicle's health and status. The color of the light indicates the severity of the issue. For example, red lights mean to stop and take immediate action, while yellow or amber lights mean to proceed with caution.
