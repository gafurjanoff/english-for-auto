Lesson 21. Electrical system

TASK 1 Read and define the bolded words.

A car's electrical system is a network of components that generates, stores, and distributes electrical energy throughout the vehicle. It includes the battery, alternator, starter motor, wiring, fuses, and other parts. The electrical system powers many functions, such as the lights, entertainment system, and power windows.

TASK 2 Match the words with their function.
Words Grammatical category Function
1. Accumulator noun a. maintains the electrical output of the alternator within a safe range
2. Voltage regulator noun b. keeps moisture and foreign particles from entering your vehicle's A/C system.
3. Generates verb c. converts mechanical energy from the engine into electrical energy
4. Generator noun d. converts motion-based power
5. Battery noun e. starts the engine, powers electrical components
6. Lights noun f. provides a variety of functions to keep the driver and passengers entertained
7. Entertainment system noun g. illuminates the road ahead and make the car visible to other drivers
