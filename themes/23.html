Lesson 23. Understanding measuring devices

TASK 1 23. Listen and comprehend the information.

Cars are equipped with several measuring devices to monitor performance, safety, and overall functionality. The measuring devices in a car serve various important purposes to ensure the vehicle operates safely, efficiently, and reliably. The importance of measuring devices in a car lies in their ability to ensure the vehicle operates efficiently, safely, and reliably.
