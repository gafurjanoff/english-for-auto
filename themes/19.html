Lesson 19. Car crankshaft mechanism

TASK 1 Read and define the bolded words.

A car's crankshaft is a mechanical component that converts the pistons' linear motion into a rotating motion, which powers the vehicle's wheels. The crankshaft is located near the bottom of the engine and is connected to the pistons by connecting rods.
