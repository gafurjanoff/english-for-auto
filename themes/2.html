TASK 5 "PERSONAL PROFILE"

Objective:
Enhance speaking skills by sharing personal information in a structured format, practicing introductions, and learning to ask follow-up questions.

Materials Needed:
- "Personal Profile" worksheet (optional) with prompts
- Timer (for timing each part of the activity)

1. Warm-Up: "Quick Introductions" (5 minutes)
Ask each student to share their name, where they are from, and one interesting fact about themselves in one sentence. Encourage them to listen carefully to each other, as there will be follow-up questions later.

2. Pair/Group Interviews (10–15 minutes)

Instructions:
Pair up students (or make small groups of three). Each student should interview their partner(s) using a set of prompts to help them talk about themselves.

Prompts:
- Basic information: name, age, where you’re from
- Favorites: favorite hobby, book, movie, or music genre
- Dreams and goals: what do you want to do in the future? any dream job?
- Family & friends: describe a family member or friend who inspires you.
- Fun fact: something unusual or unique about yourself

Roles:
- One student is the interviewer and asks questions.
- The other is the speaker who responds.
- After a few minutes, they switch roles.
